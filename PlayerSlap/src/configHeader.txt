# slapself - Set whether people can slap themselves or not 
# slapdefault - The default slap type 
# slaptypes - The definition of the slap types 
#   1 - The name of a slap type 
#     	health (integer) - The health removed when a player is slapped 
#     	mustaccept (true/false) - Whether the player must accept the slap or not 
#		lighning (true/false) - Whether the player should be struck by lightning or not 
#		smoke (integer) - The radius of the smoke effect (0 for no smoke) 
#		messages: | $Giver becomes the slap giver; $Slapped become the person slapped; $Default becomes the default messages; $None for nothing  
			broadcast: $Giver slapped $Slapped - The message broadcast to the server 
			personal: You were slapped by $Giver - The message sent to the slapped player 
			death: $Default - The addition message broadcast if the player is slapped to death 
#		mobs - The mobs to spawn near the player (ENTITYTYPE: integer)
